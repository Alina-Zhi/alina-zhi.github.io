About

Hi, I'm Xuzhe Zhi

My research lies at the intersection of machine learning, healthcare, and human-centered AI. I study how learning systems can be designed to work reliably with complex, real-world data—such as medical images, physiological signals, and clinical text—while accounting for uncertainty, fairness, and real-world constraints. Through academic research and clinical collaborations, my goal is to develop AI methods that not only advance modeling performance but also support trustworthy and equitable decision-making in high-stakes settings.

  • Languages: Python, Java, JavaScript, SQL, HTML/CSS
  • ML/DL Frameworks: PyTorch, PyTorch Lightning, TensorFlow, Hugging Face Transformers, DALI
  • Tools & Platforms: Docker, Kubernetes, MongoDB, AWS, Git, TensorBoard, LangChain
  • Research Areas: Computer Vision, Time-Series Analysis, Physics-Informed ML, Medical AI
